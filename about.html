KAYT

Our goal is to help you decide what course you should take for senior high or college, this is one of your biggest turning point in your life so you should think deeply about your decision, I hope KAYT will help you in your decisions, Goodluck Traveler

VISION

We dream of a future where Senior high school students are passionate and excelling on their chosen Strand and Track. We believe that if Senior high school students truly understand and love their chosen track, we will see a dramatic improvement of our economy that will lead towards a better future.

MISSION

We are securing a future wherein Senior high school students are confident, certain, and positive about their chosen Track and Strand.
We hope to help them by providing an in-depth information about Strands available in Senior high school here in Philippines before they choose one so that they will have the wisdom to choose what is the best path to take.
